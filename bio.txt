Andreia Gaita is a senior C#/C++ developer at GitHub, leading the team that builds the GitHub for Unity and GitHub for Visual Studio extensions. For the past 16 years Andreia has been involved in the development of game engines, cross-platform applications, services, libraries, embedding browser engines, creating bindings, and making tools that help developers be successful. She hails from the sunny city of Lisbon, Portugal, and currently lives in the rainy city of Copenhagen, Denmark, where she bikes a lot.
